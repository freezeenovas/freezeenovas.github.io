Cookies do not damage your computer and they cannot be used to discover the identity of a user. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie. This enables you to decide if you want to accept it or not and whether you are required to accept the cookie to view certain content. FreezeNova may use cookies to “persist” a user session — that is, to maintain connection with a user as he or she moves through an application or series of web pages.
