Crazy Parking Fury

In Crazy Parking Fury, you’ll sit behind the wheel of a supercar looking for a parking spot in a quaint beach town. There are 5 levels for you to unlock, each one with different characteristics and parking spots to master. The controls are simple – use WASD or the arrow keys to control your car and C to change the camera angle.

Watch out for the police – they’re just waiting for you to cause a disturbance and get you off the streets. You can be slow and careful or fast and accurate, but watch out for other vehicles when looking for a parking spot.

Crazy Parking

With 25 distinct levels, Crazy Parking will thoroughly test your ability to steer and park without bumping into anything. Collect all the stars along the way to boost your score and master all the levels, but watch out for traffic cones and other obstacles that will stop you in your tracks. Maneuver perfectly and you’ll win, but touch anything and it’s game over for you!

You can use WASD or arrow keys to control your car – no other controls necessary. Crazy Parking is a simple and fun game that will keep you entertained for some time. Don’t try to rush it though – it requires caution and finesse to handle the more complex parking levels.

Monster Truck City Parking

Have you ever wondered what it would be like to park a monster truck? Well, now you don’t have to – Monster Truck City Parking takes you into a sprawling metropolis and puts you in the front seat of an enormous monster truck that you will need to carefully park to pass each level. Use WASD or arrow keys for moving, space for handbrake, and C to change the camera angle.

For completing each level, you’ll receive money that you can then use to upgrade your monster truck. Upgrade your engine, brakes, and maximum speed to beat the harder levels in record time. Be careful – maneuvering a monster truck isn’t as easy as you might think! With time, you’ll get better at it, being able to beat more and more difficult levels. Don’t forget to upgrade your monster truck – it’ll help you beat the time limit in later stages.

If you thought parking a monster truck was difficult, try parking a tank in a busy dockyard! There are as many as 44 levels for you to accomplish, and you’ll be rewarded with cash for completing each. Time is of the essence here – the faster you reach your goal and park perfectly, the more stars you’ll receive for a given level. Each level is harder than the previous one.

Monoa City Parking

Monoa City Parking is yet another game where the objective is to park your car – but this time, you can fully customize your vehicle as well. Like with other parking games, the controls are simple – use WASD or arrow keys to move around, space for handbrake, and C to change camera angle. You can also press H to honk your horn.
