Top 6 Mini Games Unblocked You’ll Find at FreezeNova

Can’t decide which game to choose? Why not try one of the Top Mini Games Unblocked from FreezeNova’s website:

Tap Tap Shots

Dunk as many slams as possible, but don’t let the time run out or your combo will reset! Tap Tap Shots is a simple yet highly addictive game that will test your reaction time and dexterity. By simply clicking or tapping the screen, you’ll bounce the ball towards the hoop. Try to get the ball in, and the hoop will quickly re-appear on the other side of the screen at a random height. Don’t let this disorient you, as you’ll only have a few seconds to get the ball to the other goal. Compete with your friends and see who can get the highest score!

Geometry Rash

Do you have the patience to beat Geometry Rash? This game is all about quick reflexes, as you’ll need to avoid obstacles by jumping with your character at just the right moment. It might sound simple at first, but the smallest mistake means you’ll have to start right back from the start. Collect stars as you play and spend them in the main menu to unlock new characters. Use your left mouse button, the W key, or simply tap your screen if you’re playing on mobile to jump.

House Painter

Let’s play something more relaxing this time - House Painter is a clam puzzle game where your goal is to move a sponge soaked with paint across all four outside walls of a house, avoiding obstacles and covering all the white space with color. Each painted house can be then built in your city, and upgraded using the money you earned for painting them. Use the mouse, WASD, or arrow keys to move the sponge around, or simply swipe with your finger if you’re playing on mobile.

Love Tester

Love Tester is a simple game that will let you put in the names of two people and see how compatible they are with each other, based purely on their names. It’s like those old TV games where you sent an SMS message with two names and a compatibility meter would appear on the screen - but this one’s for free!

Stickman Swing

Let’s get back to dexterity games - swing your character around using grappling hooks in Stickman Swing and try to reach the finish line in each level without hitting obstacles that could bounce you off-screen. Each new level will be more difficult than the previous one, and you’ll have to carefully plan your trajectory to make it to the next. As you play, you’ll unlock new character skins as well.

Tower of Colors

Finally, a fun and engaging puzzle game - Tower of Colors is a colorful 3D game where your goal is to destroy a tower composed of colored blocks with your cannon. The trick is, your cannonballs are colored as well, and you can only destroy matching-color blocks with them. Can you beat all the levels and get to the end?
