Heist Escape

In Heist Escape, you’ll play as an ambitious bank robber – perhaps a little too ambitious, since the building is filled with patrolling guards who’ll catch you if they see you. Sneak around the bank and find all the money bags, then make your escape while avoiding the guards. Heist Escape is simple in concept, but will still provide a challenge with 30 distinct levels, each next one harder than the previous.

Can you beat all the levels and become rich? Try your best in Heist Escape, but don’t let the guards see you.

Thief.ro

This 3D escape game will test your dexterity and puzzle-solving skills – in Thief.ro, you’ll become a thief trying to escape from the scene of crime. Each generated level will feature different obstacles for you to avoid. Watch out for the policemen and collect the key that will unlock the doors to freedom.

You can play with arrow keys or WASD, or you can play with your mouse. Navigate through the levels, find the exit, and don’t let anyone see you.

Granny

Granny is an adrenaline-filled mixture of horror and escape games, placing you alone with a bloodthirsty Granny who’s out to get you. Find your way out of a variety of levels, but don’t let Granny see you, or else she’ll make short work of you. Sneak around, find the exit, and avoid Granny. Use the arrow keys or WASD to move around, left shift to run, and space to jump. There is no way to defend yourself against the granny, so make sure she doesn’t spot you walking around.

Soul Essence Adventure

In this old-school side-scroller, you’ll have to hack your way through hordes of enemies while navigating levels full of obstacles. Soul Essence Adventure is a Castlevania-inspired title with RPG elements, where each enemy you defeat will grant you experience points that will help you level up your character. Each level increases your stats, making you stronger and more powerful – which you’ll need to beat the game’s difficult bosses.

Manage your inventory and look for new, powerful weapons hidden across the levels. Use your transformation power to beat the strongest enemies, but be careful – it has a time limit and a cooldown, so make sure to save it when really necessary. Move around with WASD or arrow keys, jump with Space, use E to interact, T to transform, and Tab or I to open your inventory.

Dragon Escape

Play as an adorable tiny dragon in Dragon Escape and navigate across platforming levels, avoiding enemies and trying to reach the end. Each level will be more difficult than the previous one, forcing you to work on your reaction times to beat them all. The game may look simple at first, but don’t let it fool you – the later levels will definitely prove challenging. Use WASD or the arrow keys to move around and space to jump. Avoid the enemies – your little dragon doesn’t have a way to defend himself!

Blocky Parkour Ninja

If you ever dreamt of becoming a ninja, Blocky Parkour Ninja is exactly the game for you. Navigate across platforming levels and jump from platform to platform – but watch out for obstacles and enemies that will try to halt your progress. Be fast, since each level is timed. 60 unique levels are waiting for you – can you beat them all and become the world’s most skilled ninja warrior?

Don’t let the early levels fool you – the platforming gets much harder as you get further into the game, so you’ll need to improve your skills if you want to beat the game.

Crazy Ball Adventures

Can you steer a ball into the finish line without falling off the edges? In Crazy Ball Adventures, you’ll have to carefully navigate with your ball and keep your balance while jumping from platform to platform and collecting stars. 30 unique levels wait for you in this game, each one introducing more difficult obstacles and narrower platforms. Watch out for the camera rotating – it might knock you off-balance after making a turn. Use the arrow keys or WASD to steer your ball, spacebar to jump, and left shift to activate a power up after collecting one.
